Thanks for agreeing to present on -date-. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Aim to keep your presentation to ~10 minutes, and leave time for ~5 mins of discussion/questions for the audience.

  • Keep in mind that not everyone is an expert on the topic, so be sure to provide context (why is this important?) and basic explanations where necessary.

  • Have a minimum goal of conveying the main points presented in the paper, as well as touching on main figures/numbers/etc. Your criticism would be an enhanced goal, which will likely come up in discussion.

  • Try consulting Mindshare, or asking around if you are having trouble picking an article.
